About Us

DMT Trailers, was founded in 2015 by Mr. Dharmpal Yadav, Founder & Chairman of the DMT United Group. DMT Trailers is one of the biggest brands of trailers and truck bodies in India. The manufacturing of several Articulated and Rigid categories Trailers, Tipping Trailers, Bulkers, Containers, Tankers etc. began operation in 2015.

Our headquarters in Pragpura, Rajasthan, has a monthly production capacity of 2000 trailers. Currently we are producing 700-800 units per month and delivering across India. Providing best quality, our devoted R&D team conducts the greatest research possible on product development.

We have a production area of more than 5 lac square feet, 1120 onsite engineers, and 1305 people overall in India. More than 10000 satisfied customers use DMT's B2B and B2C products And services in the Indian market.
